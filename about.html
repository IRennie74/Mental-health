ABOUT

Attenition-deficit/hyperactive disorder (ADHD) is a very common mental dissorder among children. Although ADHD is mostly among children there are still many adults that are affected. Even though ADHD is very common, some people believe that people that every person that has ADHD is "mentally challenged" or they think negatively about the dissorder. Even though many of the most succesful people in the world to have ADHD. Did you know that the following celebritys have ADHD, Bill Gates, justin Timberlake, Leonardo da Vinci, Michael Phelps, and even Albert Einstein.

There are many different types of ADHD, one of the major ADHD types is ADHD, combined type. This type of ADHD is the most common type, it includes impulsive and hyperactive behaviors as well as inattention and distractibility. Another common ADHD is ADHD, impulsive/hyperactive type. This ADHD type is the least common and it is impulsive and hyperactive behaviors without inattention and distractibility. The third main type of ADHD is ADHD, inattentive and distractible type. When having this disorder you have inattention and distractibility without hyperactivity.

Even though the cause of ADHD is unknown does not mean that people with ADHD are harmful in any shape or form. People with ADHD may often feel left out or uncomfortable and thats why we have to help everyone out and to make sure not to treat anyone differently. It is possible for anyone to get ADHD so we all have to be mindful,kind and understanding.
